## Short summary

Frieren is the main protagonist of *Frieren: Beyond Journey's End*. She was the mage of the Hero Party and traveled with Himmel, Eisen, and Heiter to defeat the Demon King. After Himmel's death, she began a new journey with Fern and Stark to reach Aureole and speak with Himmel once again.
